Who are we ?

We are small, but strong team

TheRemindersNetwork was started in 2014 with the aim of spreading beneficial Islamic Knowledge among Humanity and combating the negative stereotypes of Islam found on the internet and in the media.

The Network consists of a number of different YouTube channels sharing Islamic Reminders & Lectures from various speakers.

Since its creation it has grown significantly to having over 200,000 Total Subscribers & over 19 Million total views.
